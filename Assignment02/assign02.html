Vehicle Management System



A connected vehicle database management system gathers and analyzes real-time data from vehicle sensors, GPS, and communication modules, covering aspects like location, speed, fuel use, and maintenance. It also interacts with insurance companies to provide driving data for risk assessments and with emergency services to relay critical information quickly in case of incidents. The system must ensure data integrity, security, and scalability while being compatible with cloud services and edge devices, enabling advanced analytics, remote diagnostics, and over-the-air updates.

Channel Predictable Free Space Optical Communication System using Machine Learning


We have proposed a system that implements a channel predictable free space optical communications system using intensity modulation and direct detection of the signal at a data rate of about 2.5 Gbps and an input power of 10dBm. The Signal is transmitted over a link length of 2 Km. The Attenuation of the channel is varied according to different weather conditions including clear sky, haze, mist, fog and rainfall and the effects are studied. Six Machine Learning algorithms are compared using Root mean square parameter where Linear regression model predicts the received power most approximately amongst all other algorithms used with a RMSE of 0.0315.

Skills Used: 5G Communications, Machine Learning, Optical communication
