Our Favorite Books

Book of the Month 1

A classic novel in the acclaimed Narnia Series by C.S Lewis which takes us on a journey of Eustace Scrubb in the magical world of Narnia. As fan favourite Prince Caspian makes an emphatic return to the series alongside some devious mysteries.

Book of the month 2

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets follows Harry's journey as he returns to Hogwarts to try and battle the monster hidden behind the Chamber of Secrets. Will he be able to save Hogwarts and his friends from grave danger?

Book of the Month 3

R.L Stine returns with another creepy and terrifying supernatural mystery involving perhaps the most dangerous living dummy terrorizing the author's and reader's imaginations. Is it finally the night where the Living Dummy Prevails or is someone waiting on the other end to stop him dead in his tracks?
